Summer project

Camping Josée

During the summer Ten Noey resolutely goes outside, on the street, on squares and in parks. We save neither effort nor time (but are
saving on our money) to set up a summer operation where on the one hand the wealth of Sint-Joost-ten-Node comes to light
(local artists get a stage, nice unknown
places are opened up, neighbors are invited on the street
to of to get in touch with each other.

The summer project used to be called:
Building the Summer of Sint-Joost, where we have children and let adults get to work with all possible (recycled)
building materials. Like this they formed a boring park into a pleasant
place to hangout, or did they build huts for the children could
climb up or could hide in. Since the corona summer
of 2020, we keep Camping Josée open during the summer.
Josée is a fictitious figure who invites everyone who does not go on holiday
to her at the campsite for a beer,
some music, playing fun and of course: for nice chats on the terrace.
